Moving clips

In the static version, a clipPath is defined as a rectangle. The clipPath is applied to the circle, so that only the top half of the circle is rendered.


<svg version="1.1" xmlns="http://www.w3.org/2000/svg" xmlns:xlink="http://www.w3.org/1999/xlink">
  <defs>
    <clipPath id="cut-off-bottom">
      <rect x="0" y="0" width="200" height="100" />
    </clipPath>
  </defs>

  <circle cx="100" cy="100" r="100" clip-path="url(#cut-off-bottom)" />
</svg>

In the first dynamic version, the clipPath rectangle is animated, moving up and down.

In the second dynamic version, the circle is animated, moving up and down. The clipPath rectangle moves in context with the circle.
